Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

At FAH AI, we believe in a private web. Our privacy policy is simple: we do not track you.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data from visitors to this app. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts.

2. Server Logs

The only information processed is standard technical data required to deliver our app to you. When you use it, our servers automatically log your Internet Protocol (IP) address.

Purpose: This is used solely for security purposes (e.g., preventing DDoS attacks) and to ensure the BitBracket app functions correctly.

Retention: These logs are temporary and are automatically deleted after 30 days.

Access: We do not use this information to identify individuals or link it to other data.

3. Third-Party Services

We host our app using BitBracket. While it facilitates the delivery of our app, we have configured our settings to ensure no unnecessary data is harvested.

4. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal information (beyond transient IP logs), we do not have a database of your personal details to provide, modify, or delete. If you have questions regarding our infrastructure, you may contact us at support@fah-ai.com.

5. Future Changes

If our data practices change in the future, this page will be updated before any new collection begins.
